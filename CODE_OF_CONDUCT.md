# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.
Examples of positive behavior include constructive feedback, empathy, clear
technical disagreement, and respect for different experience levels.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, doxxing, sexualized attention,
and deliberate disruption are unacceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to `lee@leebase.com`. Reports will be
reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may
remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors whose behavior is
inconsistent with this standard.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project in
public community spaces.
